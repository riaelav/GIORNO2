Focaccia with caramelised onions, Cramberries and Brie

Wow your taste buds with this festive focaccia appetizer topped with sweet caramelised onions, tarts cramberries and creamy brie. It will become your new favorite holiday appetizer.

Ingredients

For the focaccia:

  • 1/2 cup warm water
  • 1/2 cup warm milk
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1 tsp active dry yeast
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/4 cup olive oil

For the topping:

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 large onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup balsamic vinegar
  • 1/2 cup fresh cranberries
  • 1/2 cup brie cheese, cubed
